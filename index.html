WARNING - Investment Scams using Crypto-Currency are on the rise

Criminals will foster a romance or a friendship while encouraging you to invest more and more of your funds to earn a higher rate of return. They'll also create fake websites and financial statements, and tell you to lie to your bank and family.

If an investment opportunity sounds too good to be true, it likely is. Be cautious of get rich quick schemes.

Think twice before transferring money from your accounts to an unknown crypto investment.
